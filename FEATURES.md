## Intellectual Life Module (Study, Reading, Writing, Research)
**Why:** Brazilian Catholic families need tools for intellectual growth (Fides et Ratio). Students, researchers, writers, and lifelong learners need study methods (active learning, metacognition), reading strategies (Mortimer Adler), note-taking (Zettelkasten), writing project tracking, and integration of Catholic intellectual tradition (Aquinas, Chesterton, Lewis) with Brazilian academic context (ABNT, CAPES, CNPq).

### Study Methods & Techniques
- [ ] **Study Session Tracker** - Pomodoro (25/5), deep work (90-120 min), time blocking, energy levels
- [ ] **Active Learning** - Feynman technique, active recall, elaboration, dual coding
- [ ] **Spaced Repetition** - Anki integration, Ebbinghaus forgetting curve, flashcard decks
- [ ] **Memory Techniques** - Mnemonics, memory palace (Method of Loci), chunking, peg system
- [ ] **Metacognition Journal** - Self-monitoring ("How did I learn this?"), strategy reflection
- [ ] **Study Environment** - Physical setup (desk, lighting), digital (distraction blocking, app limits)

### Reading Strategies (Mortimer Adler)
- [ ] **Reading Log** - Books, articles, progress (%), annotations, quotes, ratings
- [ ] **Four Levels of Reading** - Elementary, inspectional (skimming), analytical, syntopical
- [ ] **SQ3R Method** - Survey, Question, Read, Recite, Review with templates
- [ ] **Reading Lists** - Great Books, Catholic tradition, personal curriculum, yearly goals
- [ ] **Active Reading** - Annotation systems (highlight colors, margin notes, questions)
- [ ] **Speed vs. Deep Reading** - Discernment per text type (academic vs. literature vs. technical)
- [ ] **Commonplace Book** - Collect quotes, insights, reflections across all reading

### Note-Taking Systems
- [ ] **Zettelkasten** - Atomic notes (one idea each), unique IDs, bidirectional links, index notes
- [ ] **Cornell Notes** - Cues column, notes column, summary section, recite from cues
- [ ] **Feynman Notes** - Explain concept simply, identify gaps, simplify further
- [ ] **Outline Method** - Hierarchical structure (I, A, 1, a), good for lectures
- [ ] **Mind Mapping** - Visual radial structure, colors, branches, concept connections
- [ ] **Tool Integration** - Obsidian (bidirectional links), Notion (databases), Logseq (outliner)

### Writing & Academic Projects
- [ ] **Writing Project Tracker** - Essays, research papers, books, dissertation with milestones
- [ ] **Daily Writing Habit** - Word count goals, streaks, Morning Pages (Julia Cameron)
- [ ] **Writing Process** - Pre-writing (outline), drafting (shitty first drafts), revising, editing
- [ ] **Essay Structure** - Introduction (hook, context, thesis), body (topic sentences), conclusion
- [ ] **Academic Writing** - Formal register, evidence-based arguments, literature integration
- [ ] **Publishing Tracker** - Journal submissions, peer review status, conference abstracts
- [ ] **Brazilian Academic Writing** - Formal Portuguese (você/third person), avoid colloquialisms

### Research Methods
- [ ] **Research Question Formulator** - Broad topic → narrow question, PICO for health
- [ ] **Literature Review** - Database search (Google Scholar, Scopus, SciELO), screening, synthesis
- [ ] **Source Evaluation** - Currency, relevance, authority, accuracy, purpose (CRAAP test)
- [ ] **Citation Management** - Zotero integration, import PDFs, automatic bibliographies
- [ ] **Research Ethics** - Plagiarism avoidance, academic integrity, human subjects (IRB/CONEP)
- [ ] **Primary vs. Secondary** - Distinguish sources, peer-reviewed vs. popular

### Brazilian Academic Context
- [ ] **ABNT Norms** - NBR 6023 (references), NBR 6024 (numbering), NBR 14724 (theses)
- [ ] **CAPES Integration** - Periodicals Portal access, program quality (grades 1-7)
- [ ] **CNPq Opportunities** - Research funding (PIBIC, PQ), scholarship tracking
- [ ] **SciELO Database** - Scientific Electronic Library Online (Latin America) search

- [ ] **Academic Culture** - Strictu sensu (master's/doctorate), defesa de tese, bancas
