# Roadmap

## Vision

**Goal:** Empower families to take control of their household management through a single, privacy-focused platform.

## Core Pillars

The Doce Lar application is built around **12 pillars** organized into **4 major groups** that work together to help families manage their daily lives:

---

### GROUP1: Finance, Legal & Estate

#### 1. Budget Control
**Why:** Financial clarity leads to better decisions. Every family deserves to understand their spending, aligned with Catholic stewardship principles.

- Expense tracking with installments, PIX/boleto integration
- Multiple payment methods (cards, cash, PIX)
- Monthly/yearly reports with Brazilian spending categories (housing, utilities, food, transport, health, education, debt, giving, discretionary)
- Budgeting methods: Zero-based, 50/30/20, envelope system
- Visual dashboards with progress tracking
- Brazilian context: Nota fiscal tracking for IR deductions。

#### 2. Debt Relief & Credit Management
**Why:** Debt enslaves families. Catholic teaching condemns usury; we help families escape predatory debt (agiotagem) via snowball/avalanche methods and Brazilian renegociação.

- Debt log: Creditors, balances, interest rates, minimum payments
- Payoff strategies: Snowball (smallest balance first), Avalanche (highest interest first)
- Brazilian renegociação: Serasa Limpa Nome, SPC programs, portabilidade de crédito
- Credit score tracking: Serasa, SPC, Quod checks
- Avoid predatory lending: Agiotagem warnings, loan shark alerts
- Debt-free milestone tracking.

#### 3. Savings & Financial Goals
**Why:** Prudent saving is Catholic stewardship. Build emergency funds, education savings, and long-term goals.

- Emergency fund tracker: 3-6 months expenses, liquid savings (poupança, CDB)
- Goal-based savings: Education (university, PROUNI/FIES backup), home down payment, vacation, wedding
- Automated savings: PIX programado, arredondamento, 13º/férias savings
- Brazilian savings vehicles: Poupança, CDB, LCI/LCA, Tesouro Direto.

#### 4. Retirement Planning
**Why:** Plan for dignified retirement, combining INSS and private pension (previdência privada) per Catholic social teaching.

- INSS tracker: Contribution time, projected benefits, age mínima (65 men/62 women)
- Private pension: VGBL/PGBL contributions, tax benefits, risk profile adjustment
- Retirement projection: 25x annual expenses rule, 4% withdrawal rate, IPCA inflation adjustment
- Healthcare cost planning for retirement.

#### 5. Youth Financial Life (18+)
**Why:** Equip young adults (jovem aprendiz, first job) to manage independent finances per Catholic values.

- First job toolkit: CLT paycheck breakdown (INSS, IR, 13º, férias), bank account setup (Nubank, Inter)
- Jovem aprendiz budget template: 50%+ savings rate, avoid lifestyle inflation
- Credit card education: Pay in full, avoid interest (200%+ yearly)
- Financial literacy checklist: Compound interest, credit score, PIX/boleto safety
- Moving out planner: 3 months expenses saved, rent budget (max 30% income), roommate cost sharing.

#### 6. Catholic Stewardship
**Why:** Everything belongs to God; we are stewards, not owners. Integrate faith with money management.

- Tithing tracker: 10% to Church (Malachi 3:10), first fruits giving
- Almsgiving tracker: Beyond tithe, help poor (Matthew 25:35-40), charitable donations
- Stewardship journal: Detachment from material goods, generosity reflections
- Ethical investing: Avoid companies contradicting Catholic values
- Family financial transparency: Joint accounts, anti-infidelity tools
- Giving integration: Sunday collection, Lenten almsgiving, disaster relief.

#### 7. Brazilian Financial Systems
**Why:** Navigate Brazil-specific systems: INSS, tax, banking, investments.

- Banking: Traditional (Itaú, Bradesco) vs digital (Nubank, Inter), PIX 24/7, boleto management
- Tax (Imposto de Renda): Annual declaration, MEI simplified tax, education/health deductions
- Investments: Tesouro Direto (Selic, IPCA+, Prefixado), CDB (100% CDI), LCI/LCA (tax-free), ações (B3)
- Labor rights (CLT): Salário mínimo, 13º, férias, FGTS, aviso prévio, rescisão
- Government benefits: Bolsa Família, BPC, auxílio tracking.

#### 8. Insurance & Real Estate
**Why:** Protect family assets per Catholic prudence (not lack of faith).

- Insurance tracker: Health (plano de saúde/SUS), life, auto, home, disability
- Real estate: Financing (financiamento) tracker, rent vs buy calculator, FGTS usage
- Policy document storage: Digital vault for insurance, contracts, IPTU.

#### 9. Legal & Estate
**Why:** Protecting family interests requires planning for the unexpected.

- Will and testament
- Power of attorney
- Legal document vault
- Emergency information

**Relationship Management:**

- New friendship builder (meetup logs, conversation starters)

- Social skills tracker (eye contact, active listening, boundaries)
- Love languages tracker (Gary Chapman: words, service, gifts, time, touch)

**Forgiveness & Reconciliation:**
- Forgiveness journal (who to forgive, steps taken, reconciliation status)
- Reconciliation steps (Matthew 18:15-17, confession, apologizing)
- Forgiveness vs. reconciliation (when to forgive without restoring relationship)
- Forgiveness prayers (Lord's Prayer: "forgive us as we forgive")
- Conflict resolution log (issue, steps taken, resolution status)

- Disagreement log (topic, positions, common ground found)

- Charity first (assume good will, avoid contempt)
- Dialogue over debate (listen to understand, not win)
- Catholic perspective (love person, not behavior; witness, not argue)

**Boundaries & Toxic Relationships:**
- Boundaries log (healthy limits set, toxic behaviors noted)
- Toxic relationship tracker (patterns, impact on family, protection steps)

- JADE avoidance (don't Justify, Argue, Defend, Explain boundaries)
- Protect children (exposure to toxic relatives/friends, safety first)

**Communication Skills:**
- Active listening log (paraphrase, validate, question, respond)

- Parish community building (ministries, small groups, retreats, fellowship)

- Grieving lost relationships (death, breakup, estrangement)

- Need for mediation (parents, couples, siblings)

**Children's Social Development (First Childhood Integration):**
